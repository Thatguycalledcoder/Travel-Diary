About Dave King

Dave King an upcoming web developer likes to gain new perspectives and see the world to really appreciate the glory of God through his creation. He is also a pythonista/ pythoneer. Although, he looks Indian which will be more prominent by the end of the year, he is actually a Ghanaian. He goes through the struggles with God lifting him up every time he falls. He is tired of this. Yes, God is always going to be there to help him up but he cannot do it anymore. He just wants it to all stop. He is a sound engineer, gamer, programmer, writer(of poems, lyrics and sometimes novels), Intern, uncle, Christian, robotics coach, and a whole lot of crazies.
He graduated from Dayspring Montessori International School for junior high school as its Assistant School prefect and robotics club member as well as Science, Maths and IT club. He graduated from St. Augustine's College as a Business Major for high school as the President of the Robotics Club, Financial Secretary of the Inkgate Club and Treasurer for the Child Rights Club. He was also the best Economics student and third best Business Management student in the school. Presently, he is a student at Ashesi University as a Computer Science major.
